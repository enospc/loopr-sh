<section class="doc-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Codex Power User</p>
  <h1>Build the loop around verification</h1>
  <p class="lead">Models will change. Your responsibility will not. These habits keep AI output aligned with intent.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="grid">
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Own outcomes</h3>
      <p>Set measurable goals and success conditions before you ask an agent to write code.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Design for verification</h3>
      <p>Tests are the core of the workflow. If it cannot be tested, it is not real.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Short loops</h3>
      <p>Optimize for fast feedback. A 15-minute loop beats a heroic weekend.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <h2>The daily loop</h2>
  <ol class="step-list">
    <li class="step-item"><span>01</span><div><strong>Clarify intent</strong><br />Rewrite the request into goals, non-goals, and constraints.</div></li>
    <li class="step-item"><span>02</span><div><strong>Build the contract</strong><br />Define interfaces, data shapes, and tests before implementation.</div></li>
    <li class="step-item"><span>03</span><div><strong>Execute a slice</strong><br />Implement one task, run tests, and log decisions.</div></li>
    <li class="step-item"><span>04</span><div><strong>Reduce entropy</strong><br />Refactor as you go. If it adds entropy, reject it.</div></li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <div class="callout">
    <strong>Human-in-the-loop is a safety valve.</strong> Review as collaboration, not judgment.
  </div>

</section>
