<section class="section" id="benefits" data-section="benefits">
  <div class="section-header">
    <p class="eyebrow">Why Loopr</p>
    <h2>Judgment over generation</h2>
    <p class="muted">The guide is clear: models change. Your responsibility does not. Loopr keeps the loop grounded in outcomes, tests, and clarity.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="grid">
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Own outcomes</h3>
      <p>Set measurable goals and success conditions before you ask an agent to write code.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Specs that compile</h3>
      <p>Requirements are testable, explicit, and include non-goals to prevent drift.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Tests are the oracle</h3>
      <p>Verification is not a phase; it is the workflow. If you cannot test it, you cannot trust it.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card">
      <h3>Reversibility wins</h3>
      <p>Architecture is a series of reversible bets backed by decision logs.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
